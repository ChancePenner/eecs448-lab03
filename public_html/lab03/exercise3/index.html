Tyranitar

Tyranitar evolves from Pupitar who evolves from Larvitar. Tyranitar is a Rock and Dark type Pokémon and loves eating rocks. Tyranitar boasts base stats of:
HP: 100
Attack: 134
Defense: 100
Sp. Atk: 95
Sp. Def: 100
Speed: 61
Height: 6' 07"
Weight: 445.3 lbs
His ability is Sand Stream, which summons a sandstorm in battle.
Tyranitar also has a mega evolution.
According to his HeartGold and SoulSilver Pokédex entries:
HeartGold: Its body can't be harmed by any sort of attack, so it is very eager to make challenges against enemies.
SoulSilver: Extremely strong, it can change the landscape. It has an insolent nature that makes it not care about others.
